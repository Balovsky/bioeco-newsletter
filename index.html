Company with more than 100 years of tradition

Bioeco is a brand with traditions dating back to 1927, which successfully combines the best traditions of classic craftsmanship with modernity and concern for the environment. Made from the finest quality natural leathers and vegan skins, our footwear represents timeless style and exceptional comfort. All production processes take place in one location, which allows us to strictly control the quality and raw materials at each stage of production.
